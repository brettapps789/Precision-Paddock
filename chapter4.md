# Chapter 4: The Autonomous Herdsman: Livestock and Yield Monitoring

## Intelligence on the Move

In the vast expanses of the Australian interior, monitoring livestock and predicting crop yields has traditionally required hundreds of man-hours. In 2026, the Sovereign Aussie AI Workforce has automated this oversight. Chapter 4 explores the deployment of "Herdsman-Agents"—edge-AI systems that use computer vision and multispectral imaging to track every head of cattle and every acre of wheat.

### The Monitoring Stack
1.  **Computer Vision at the Edge:** Farm Nodes process drone feeds locally to detect signs of illness in livestock or pest infestations in crops before they spread.
2.  **Yield Prediction Models:** Using localized historical data from the Aussie Brain, the workforce predicts harvest windows with 98% accuracy.
3.  **Sovereign Alerts:** Direct integration with the Aussie AI Taskmaster ensures that if a perimeter fence is breached or a crop reaches peak maturity, the human architect is notified instantly on a secure, Australian-hosted channel.
