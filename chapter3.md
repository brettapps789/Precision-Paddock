1.  **Localized Evapotranspiration Models:** Global weather models often miss the micro-climates of regional Australia. Our Farm Nodes run localized models that adjust irrigation schedules based on onsite sensor data, saving up to 40% more water than traditional systems.
2.  **Closed-Loop Accountability:** All water usage data is logged exclusively on the Aussie Node. This ensures that your farm's resource strategy remains a commercial secret, protected from global data-harvesting competitors.
3.  **Autonomous Fail-Safes:** Using the Aussie AI Taskmaster logic, the irrigation system includes autonomous rollbacks. If a leak is detected or a sensor fails, the system enters a 'Sovereign Safe' state, preserving both the crop and the resource.
